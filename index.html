About Me

Hi! I'm Vand and I create tech solutions to solve problems. I always have liked technology since my childhood, at first, I wanted to be a game developer but I was delighted with the experience of solving real problems and impacting people's lives.

My main focus these days is building reliable solutions that can cause a good experience for clients.
